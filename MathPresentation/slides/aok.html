Mathematics and AoK by Nick The Turtle

It can be said that most if not all AoK are just mathematics applied to special cases.
Natural Sciences
  • All natural sciences are ultimately areas of physics (chemistry as the study of physics pertaining to molecular interactions, biology as the study of physics pertaining to life, earth science as the study of physics pertaining to the earth, astronomy as the study of physics pertaining to the heavens, etc.).
  • All laws of physics are basically mathematical equations modelling certain variables under certain situations.
Social Sciences and the Arts
  • The social sciences include history, psychology, economics, and other studies of human behaviors and human societies; this area of science can be argued to be applying the natural sciences (and thus mathematics) on human behavior, past, present, and future.
  • The arts is concerned with aesthetics as perceived by humans and is thus a special case of the social sciences.
Ethics and Religion
  • Ethics is the study of resolving morality, and religion is a system of belief that typically includes a deity or another supernatural concept and that typically stipulates actions that one must follow.
  • If one's belief in ethics and religion does not incorporate a non-material element, then it could be said math encompasses ethics and religion since they become a part of the study of the phyisical world.
  • However, if one believes in a non-material element, it may not be possible to use math to explain that non-material element.
