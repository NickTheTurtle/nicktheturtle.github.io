Mathematics is the study of the abstract, such as quantity and space, through the use of reasoning. There is no accepted definition for math.

Applied mathematics is the study of applying mathematical concepts and methods to real life.
Pure mathematics, as opposed to applied mathematics, is the field of mathematics concerned with extremely abstract concepts, those that are not necessarily easily applied to the real world. Pure mathematics is only explored to satisfy human curiosity.
